Complete Denture Fundamentals

Anatomical Landmarks

  • Maxillary:
    • Incisive papilla
    • Rugae
    • Vibrating line
    • Hamular notch
  • Mandibular:
    • Retromolar pad
    • Buccal shelf
    • Mylohyoid ridge

Border Molding

  • Define peripheral seal
  • Green stick compound
  • Muscle trimming

Denture Teeth Selection

  • Anterior:
    • Size (interpupillary width)
    • Shape (face form)
    • Color (skin tone, age)
  • Posterior:
    • Anatomic vs non-anatomic
    • 0° vs 20° vs 33° teeth

Components

  • Minor connector
  • Rests:
    • Occlusal
    • Cingulum

    • Circumferential
    • Bar (RPI, RPA)

Design Principles

  • Kennedy Classification:
    • Class I: Bilateral edentulous
    • Class II: Unilateral edentulous
    • Class III: Unilateral bounded
    • Class IV: Anterior bounded

    • Identify undercuts

Occlusal Concepts

Complete Denture Occlusion

  • Balanced occlusion:
    • Bilateral contacts
    • Protrusive/working balance
  • Neutrocentric concept:
    • Flat plane
    • Neutral position
  • Lingualized occlusion:
    • Max lingual cusps contact
    • Reduced lateral forces

Fixed Prosthodontics Occlusion

  • Mutually protected:
    • Canine guidance
    • Posterior disclusion
  • Group function:
    • Multiple contacts
    • For compromised canines
  • Centric relation:
    • Reproducible position
    • Basis for reconstruction

Troubleshooting Prostheses

Complete Dentures

  • Sore spots:
    • Selective pressure areas
    • Relieve with bur
  • Loose fit:
    • Reline/rebase
    • Denture adhesives
  • Fractures:
    • Midline fractures
    • Repair with acrylic

Fixed Prostheses

  • Debonding:
    • Improper preparation
    • Cementation failure
  • Fractured porcelain:
    • Repair with composite
    • Replace restoration
  • Recurrent caries:
    • Marginal leakage
    • New restoration needed

Prosthesis Maintenance

  • Daily care:
    • Brushing with soft brush

    • Overnight soaking
  • Professional care:
    • Annual examinations
    • Relines as needed
    • Oral cancer screening

Fixed Prostheses

  • Home care:
    • Floss threaders
    • Interproximal brushes
    • Non-abrasive toothpaste
  • Professional care:
    • Marginal integrity checks
    • Occlusal evaluation
    • Radiographic monitoring
